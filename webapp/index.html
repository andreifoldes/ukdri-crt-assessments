During the workshop you'll answer these questionnaires twice so we can look at their test–retest reliability — in plain terms, whether a questionnaire gives you a similar score when you answer it on two separate occasions. Your ID is simply what lets us match your two sets of answers to each other, without knowing who you are. We'll delete the collected data once the workshop ends.

Your responses are anonymous. This ID is the only thing that links your attempt 1 and attempt 2 results, so please note it and use the same ID on both attempts.

Your anonymous responses are saved securely to the organisers' spreadsheet so we can measure the questionnaires' reliability.
